The ability to communicate effectively in English is a crucial factor in making the most of the educational and work opportunities that await you.

Since 1961, ELS has been the leading provider of English language programs on college campuses and exciting urban centers in the United States. Our English for Academic Purposes program is now available at ELS Centers in Australia, Canada, India, Malaysia, Panama, as well as more than sixty different locations in the United States alone.

ELS offers you a variety of programs to help you acquire the English skills that are essential today for academic and professional success; Experiences full of discovery and adventure, new friendships and cultures, learning and achievements.

ENGLISH FOR ACADEMIC PURPOSES

For those students who wish to be admitted to a university or institution of higher education, upon successful completion of Level 109 or 112 of the English for Academic Purposes (EAP) program, they receive an official certificate recognized by more than 650 universities and educational institutions confirming that they have acquired the command of the English language required for admission.

In addition to focusing on oral comprehension, conversational practice, reading, writing, pronunciation skills and English grammar, ELS's English for Academic Purposes curriculum also develops skills in other areas such as:

  • Critical thinking
  • Presentations
  • Taking notes
  • Academic Research
  • and much more…

Morning classes consist of:

  • Conversational practice and structure in communication or language studies
  • Enrichment of vocabulary (Levels 101 - 109) or classes to improve skills (only for Master level)
  • A class in our Language and Technology Center (LTC)

The afternoon classes are devoted to reading and writing.

Interact in America! Regularly give you the opportunity to meet with native speakers of English and listen to guest speakers.

GENERAL ENGLISH

For anyone who is looking to improve the English skills they need to communicate accurately and effectively.

The program includes intensive skill development in the following areas:

  • Oral comprehension
  • Pronunciation
  • Grammar
  • Conversational practice
  • vocabulary

Morning classes consist of:

  • Conversational practice and structure in communication or language studies
  • Vocabulary Enrichment
  • Class to improve skills in the morning instead of Vocabulary Enrichment (Master levels)
  • A class in our Language and Technology Center (LTC)

At all levels, afternoons are dedicated to improving skills, such as idioms used in the United States, American filmmaking, news and American culture.

Interact in America! Regularly give you the opportunity to meet with native speakers of English and listen to guest speakers. The conversation is practiced using practical, real-life English.

* The exact courses offered at each center may vary.

SEMI-INTENSIVE ENGLISH

For those who want to learn English while exploring local cities and sites of interest. This allows them to practice English outside the classroom.

This program includes the development of important language skills that are necessary to be able to communicate accurately and effectively:

  • Oral comprehension
  • Pronunciation
  • Conversational practice
  • vocabulary

The classes are only taught in the mornings and serve to build a base of skills for communication. They include:

  • Conversational practice and structure in communication or language studies
  • Vocabulary Enrichment
  • A class in our Language and Technology Center (LTC)

Interact in America! Regularly give you the opportunity to meet with native speakers of English, listen to guest speakers, and participate in local tours.

The conversation is practiced using practical, real-life English.
