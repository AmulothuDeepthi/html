Study English. Get to know. Triumph

The ability to communicate effectively in English is critical to getting the most out of the professional and teaching opportunities that await you.

Since 1961, ELS is the leading provider of English language programs at university campuses and city centers in the USA. Our English for Academic Purposes program is now available at ELS centers in Australia, Canada, India, Malaysia, and Panama, as well as more than 60 locations in the US alone.

ELS offers a variety of programs to help you achieve the English language skills so important to academic success and career today: experiences full of discovery and adventure, new friends and new cultures, learning and achievement.

ENGLISH FOR ACADEMIC OBJECTIVES

For students wishing to enter a college or university, successful completion of the Level 109 or Level 112 Academic Objectives program guarantees an official certificate recognized by more than 650 colleges and universities, confirming that you have attained proficiency in the language required for admission.

In addition to focusing on listening, speaking, reading, writing, pronunciation and grammar skills, ELS's English for Academic Purposes curriculum also develops skills in:

  • Critical Thinking
  • Introductions
  • Annotations
  • Academic research
  • and more....

Morning classes consist of the following:

  • Structure and Practice of Conversation or Language Studies
  • Vocabulary Enrichment (Levels 101 - 109) or Enhanced Skills Classes (Master's only)
  • A lesson in our Language Technology Center (LTC)

Afternoon classes are dedicated to reading and writing.

Contact America! offers periodic opportunities for meetings with native speakers of English and lectures with guest speakers.

GENERAL ENGLISH

For anyone looking to hone their English language skills, you need to communicate accurately and effectively.

The program includes intensive skill development in:

  • Listening
  • Pronunciation
  • Grammar
  • Conversation
  • Vocabulary

Morning classes consist of the following:

  • Structure and Practice of Conversation or Language Studies
  • Vocabulary Enrichment
  • Improved Morning Enrichment Capabilities in Enrichment of Vocabulary (masters levels)
  • A class in our Language Technology Center

Afternoons at all levels are dedicated to enhanced Skills Classes, such as American Idioms, American Movies, Current Events, and American Culture. *

Contact America! offers periodic opportunities for meetings with native speakers of English and lectures with guest speakers. Conversation is practiced using practical real-world English.

* The specific courses offered at each location may vary.

SEMI-INTENSIVE ENGLISH

This program includes the development of important language skills necessary for accurate and effective communication:

  • Listening
  • Pronunciation
  • Grammar
  • Vocabulary

The classes are conducted only in the morning and build a basis for communication skills. Classes include:

  • Structure and Practice of Conversation or Language Studies
  • Vocabulary Enrichment
  • A class in our Language Technology Center

Contact America! offers periodic opportunities for encounters with native speakers of English and lectures with guest speakers as well as participation in local tours.

Conversation is practiced using practical, real-world English.
